About Us

UpShot Technologies Pvt Ltd is present in Mohali, Punjab but operates internationally, started its operations in Oct 2020. UpShot is a provider in BPO that takes up outsourcing processes. These services span over diverse areas like Medical Transcription services, Lead Generation, Sales and Support, Chat and Email support, Customer Service and Technical Support.

UpShot Technologies is a pioneer in managing business processes for companies of the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and India. The company blends process expertise, BPO, and analytical capabilities in diverse industries to provide a broad range of services.

UpShot Technologies is a provider of outsourced Medical Transcription services for clinics, and physician practices across North America and BPO services for US, Canada and UK. At UpShot, we believe in high standards, specializing in superior performance, integrity, and world class medical transcription service. UpShot provides state of the art transcription solutions that seamlessly integrate with client needs. The quality of clinical documentation is critical to patient care. The standards and methodologies behind UpShot's process enable our full-time transcriptionist and quality improvement specialists and editors to ensure high-quality medical records that enhance our clients' ability to provide quality healthcare.
